How to Use

Click to see the instruction

1. Wet hair thoroughly with warm water.
2. Apply a generous amount of shampoo and massage gently into the scalp, working through to the ends.
3. Leave on for 1-2 minutes to allow the ginseng and botanical extracts to nourish the hair and scalp.
4. Rinse thoroughly and repeat if desired.
5. For best results, follow with Ginseng Total Moisture & Nourish Conditioner.
